EQ: Emotional Intelligence





Emotional Intelligence is the ability and aptitude to understand the personal and interpersonal! While IQ is about on cognitive and analytical aspects intelligence, EQ (emotional intelligence) is the emotional and relational aspects. Qualities of EQ are self-awareness, empathy, social skills, leadership, motivation and resilience. EQ is the ability to observe emotions, differentiate between them and them use that knowledge to make better decisions for yourself and other around you. The steps that help you do that are:

  1. Attention: observing, active listening without judgement
  2. Mastery: identifying and distinguishing between emotions and relationships
  3. Mental habits: mindfulness, meditation, gratitude

Emotional Intelligence is really important for flourishing teams. A recent harvard study should that a single toxic co-worker could bring down the productivity of an entire team! 1 But beyond team performance, we are all humans - social, relational beings - emotional intelligence is important to work and live better together.
