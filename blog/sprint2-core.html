My Learning Plan





It's time to create to my learning plan for how I want to learn coding and human skills at Enspiral DevAcademy. To start of with, here are my strength and limitations:


Strengths

Creativity and lateral thinking

Openness and responsive to feedback

Quick learner

Limitations

Time management

Saying 'No'

Prefectionism


Workload and Team Management

I'm commited to prioritising my learning inspite of having other things going on my life for the next 100 days. I am to keep my inner critic in-check when I hit blockers while learning or while in a team situation. I'm also going to aim to get things done rather than have them done perfectly. I also aim to have clear and transparent communication with my facilitators and peers so they know what I'm dealing with with regards to my workload and capacity.


Seeking help

As someone who is introverted, I tend to keep issues to myself OR try and solve my own problems. I recognise that this is not always healthy and will aim to seek help if things are getting too overwhelming. On a technical side, I will aim to try and solve blockers for 20mins max before seeking external help.


My facilitators

I see my facilitators as coaches who will help me grow and accelerate my learning during DevAcademy. I expect them to push me to my best ability, while also recognising if there are things that are stopping me from achieving my goals. I also see them as my accountability-buddy who I checkin with at a regular cadence to keep me progressing.


My learning schedule

I take a few moments at the end of each sprint to identify how I can be more efficient with my learning and delivery. At the start of the sprint I skim through the curriculum and plot out the hours needed to learn the content. I then map this onto a weekly calendar alongside my other commitments. While learning coding is my priority for the next 3.5 months, I also have to progress other aspects such as university interviews and potential career opportunities. I aim to code/learn 6 days of the week with a flexible schedule during Foundations. The reason for flexibility is to get other life tasks finished and out of the way before I start bootcamp in Mid-march.
